PRODUCT UPDATE • OCT 12, 2025

Introducing the Authority Graph™ 2.0

The first semantic engine capable of measuring true B2B influence without vanity metrics.

In the B2B world, influence isn't about how many followers you have. It's about who listens to you when you speak. For too long, marketing teams have been forced to rely on consumer-grade metrics—likes, shares, and follower counts—to make enterprise-grade decisions.

Today, we're changing that.

Beyond the Follower Count

The Authority Graph™ 2.0 is designed to answer one question: Does this person shape the decisions of my target buyer?

We built a new semantic engine that analyzes:

  • Contextual Relevance: Not just "Does he talk about Tech?", but "Does he influence the Enterprise Security purchasing committee?"
  • Network Density: Who interacts with them? Are they other experts, or just bots?
  • Sentiment Quality: Are people agreeing with them, or arguing?

How It Works

Using a combination of LLMs and graph theory, we map the relationships between millions of B2B professionals. When you search for "Fintech in London," we don't just look for keywords. We look for the central nodes in that specific trust network.

What This Means for You

For our beta partners, this has meant a 3x increase in campaign ROI. By reallocating budget from "broad reach" influencers to "high authority" niche experts, they reached fewer people—but closed more deals.
