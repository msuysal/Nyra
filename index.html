Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about NYRA's intelligence engine.

How does NYRA measure influence?

+

NYRA uses a proprietary semantic engine that analyzes not just follower count, but the context, consistency, and engagement quality of an expert's content. We map their authority within specific niche topics to determine true influence.

Which platforms do you support?

+

Currently, we provide deep intelligence for LinkedIn and YouTube, as these are the primary drivers for B2B decision-making. We are actively expanding to include Twitter/X and niche industry forums.

Is the data compliant with privacy laws?

+

Absolutely. NYRA only indexes public data available on open platforms. We do not access private messages or restricted content, ensuring full compliance with GDPR and platform terms of service.

Can I export the data?

+

Yes, all plans include export capabilities. You can export influencer lists, authority scores, and contact information (where publicly available) to CSV or integrate directly with your CRM.
