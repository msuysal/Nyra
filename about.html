OUR MISSION

The Global Standard for
B2B Influencer Marketing.

We are on a mission to bring engineering precision to the chaotic world of B2B influence, mapping every key opinion leader and digital authority node.

The Vision

In the modern B2B landscape, vanity metrics are failing. Follower counts and generic engagement rates no longer predict market movement. At NYRA, we believe that true influence isn't about being loud—it's about being listened to by the right people. We map the **Key Opinion Leaders (KOLs)** and **Digital Opinion Leaders (DOLs)** who actually drive decisions.

We built the Authority Graph™ to map the intellectual resonance of **thought leaders** and **LinkedIn influencers** within specific technical sectors. By analyzing millions of semantic connections, professional citations, and decision-maker interactions, we provide companies with the intelligence layer needed to move markets with confidence.

Our Approach

We treat influence as a data science problem, not a creative one. Our team of engineers and researchers has developed a proprietary engine that identifies the true "nodes of influence"—the **B2B content creators** and **LinkedIn authorities** whose voices actually drive technical trust and procurement decisions.
