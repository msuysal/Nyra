Questions & Answers

Everything you need to know about NYRA, our philosophy, and our foundational approach to B2B authority.

Authority Intelligence is the systematic mapping and analysis of influence within specific B2B sectors. Unlike traditional influencer marketing, we bring engineering precision to **B2B influencer marketing**, focusing on technical trust and intellectual resonance rather than vanity metrics like follower counts.

Our engine maps millions of professional interactions, citations, and semantic connections to identify the true **Key Opinion Leaders (KOLs)** and **Digital Opinion Leaders (DOLs)** in any given market. It identifies not just who is loudest, but who is being listened to by decision-makers.

NYRA is an intelligence layer. While we possess deep data graphs, our value lies in the predictive analytics and activation workflows that help companies forecast the impact of their strategic messaging.

We only analyze public professional data and strictly adhere to global privacy standards. We do not track personal user data or engage in invasive surveillance; our focus is on professional authority and public intellectual footprints.
